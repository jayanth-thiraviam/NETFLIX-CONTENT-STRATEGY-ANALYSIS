Netflix Content Strategy Analysis

1. Project Overview:

This project analyzes Netflix's content catalog to uncover insights into its content strategy, audience targeting, global expansion, and content acquisition trends. Using Python-based data analysis and visualization techniques, the project transforms raw Netflix data into actionable business insights.

The analysis focuses on answering key business questions such as:

  a.What type of content dominates Netflix's catalog?
  b.How has Netflix's content library evolved over time?
  c.Which genres are most popular?
  d.Which countries contribute the most content?
  e.What audience segments does Netflix primarily target?
  f.Does Netflix focus on newly released content or older acquisitions?


2. Objectives:

  a.Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Netflix's content dataset.
  b.Identify content distribution between Movies and TV Shows.
  c.Analyze content growth trends over time.
  d.Discover the most popular genres.
  e.Evaluate Netflix's international content strategy.
  f.Understand audience targeting through content ratings.
  g.Generate business recommendations using data-driven insights.

5. Key Findings

a. Content Distribution

* Movies dominate Netflix's content library.
* TV Shows form a smaller but significant portion of the catalog.

b. Global Expansion

* The United States contributes the highest number of titles.
* India is among the top content-producing countries.
* Netflix heavily invests in international content.

c. Popular Genres

* International Movies
* Dramas
* Comedies

d. Audience Targeting

* Most content is rated TV-MA and TV-14.
* Netflix primarily targets adult and young-adult audiences.

e. Content Strategy

* A large portion of content is added shortly after release.
* Indicates strong investment in recent releases and Netflix Originals.

f. TV Show Trends

* Most TV Shows have only 1–2 seasons.
* Reflects Netflix's focus on binge-friendly content.


6. Business Recommendations

1. Continue expanding international content offerings.
2. Increase investments in high-performing genres such as Drama and Comedy.
3. Maintain a balanced mix of original and licensed content.
4. Explore underserved audience segments.
5. Leverage regional content for global audience growth.
